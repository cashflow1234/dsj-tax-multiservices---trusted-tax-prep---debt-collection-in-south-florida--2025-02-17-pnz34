Essential Tax Deductions Every Small Business Owner Should Know

Maximize your small business tax savings with this comprehensive guide to essential deductions. From home office expenses like mortgage interest and utilities to business-related travel costs and vehicle expenses, learn what you can claim. The guide covers deductible equipment and supplies, professional services, education expenses, and marketing costs. Key deductions include office furniture, computers, legal fees, continuing education, and advertising expenses. Proper documentation through receipts, mileage logs, and financial statements is crucial for claiming these deductions. Using accounting software can streamline expense tracking and ensure you capture all eligible deductions throughout the year.
